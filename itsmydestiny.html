A Guardian Rises

I feel like everyone has one game. The one that scratches an important itch. For me, that is the game Destiny 2. I have spent several years collecting gear, facing down tough challenges, and making new friends. For those who are unfamiliar with the game, I want to break it down for you.

Destiny, at its core, is a looter shooter, but it's the space magic and smooth gunplay that really up the enjoymnet. You play as a resurrected warrior of light: A Guardian. And you defend the Solar System from aliens and robots! Sounds cool, right?
